ABOUT ME


I am Zeloy Molina, a junior full stack developer with passion for design and everything creative, eager for knowledge and always ready for a new challenge.


I was born and raised in the Canary Islands, lived in Barcelona several years studying industrial ans interior design. I moved to London six years ago, is here where I got in touch with the world of coding and learned in Le Wagon London how to work with Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Angular, management tools like Trello and Slack and design tools like Sketch, Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud.


During all this years living in the British capital I have been working as a bartender in several venues, where I have the opportunity of meeting people from different countries, cultures and backgrounds, doing this job I've gained a lot of experience in human relationships and enriched myself as a person. I have held management and responsibility positions, feeling very comfortable in both of them, broadening my life experience at every step.

In this moment I feel ready for a new challenge, keeping on with my philosophy of continuous learning as a way of life. The discovery of this world has supposed a life changing moment, that makes me feel reinforced and accomplished and allow that my creative passion keeps on developing
